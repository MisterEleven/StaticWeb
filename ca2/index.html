Design Concept

🌳 Vegetation Integration

Designed to preserve and integrate existing vegetation, particularly the plum tree and banana trees, as valued elements of the garden.

🍃 Leaf-Shaped Configuration

Maximizes shaded area through organic form with height variations. Two load-bearing sides: longer at back (parallel to fence), shorter at front (opening to garden).

☀️ Solar Optimization

South side: lower height for stronger shading with plum tree. North side: taller, more open configuration. Significant shade coverage, especially in early afternoon.

🏗️ Flexible Interior

Wide, open covered space (40m²) ensures adaptability. Accommodates various uses and plant positioning, including potential kiwi plant relocation along structural base.
